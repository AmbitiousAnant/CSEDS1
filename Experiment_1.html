Course Curriculum

B.Tech. in AI & Machine Learning

This is the course curriculum for Bachelor of Technology with specialization in Data Science. The program is designed for 1st year & includes theory as well as practical courses.

  1. SEMESTER I: Foundational Concepts

    • Unit 1 : Introduction to Python Programming
      Course Code: AI101
      Credits: 4
      Key Topics:
      • Basics of Python Syntax
      • Control Structures & Functions
      • Introduction to NumPy & Pandas
    • Unit 1 : Linear Algebra & Calculus
      Course Code: MTH101
      Credits: 4
      Key Topics:
      • Vectors and Matrices
      • Derivatives and Integrals
      • Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors
    • Electrical Engineering
      Course Code:EC101
      Credits: 4
      Key Topics:
      • Basic Circuit Theory
      • AC/DC Analysis
      • Other Electrical Devices

  2. SEMESTER II: Core AI Principles

    • Unit 2 : Data Structures & Algorithms
      Course Code: CS102
      Credits: 4
      Key Topics:
      • Arrays, Stacks, Queues, and Linked Lists
      • Trees and Graphs
      • Sorting and Searching Algorithms
    • Foundations of Machine Learning
      Course Code: AI102
      Credits: 3
      Key Topics:
      • Supervised vs. Unsupervised Learning
      • Linear & Logistic Regression
      • Introduction to Neural Networks
    • Unit 2 : Trignometry and Differential Equations
      Course Code:MTH102
      Credits: 4
      Key Topics:
      • Trigonometric Functions and Identities
      • Solving Differential Equations
      • Applications in Engineering Problems
